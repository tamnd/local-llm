Each guide is built around a job rather than a flag: how one 24 GB card serves
models that do not fit together, how to point an existing OpenAI-compatible
client at the gateway over the tailnet, and how to push the dense tier toward its
throughput ceiling. They assume `llmgw` is already running and reachable on the
box.
